💌 A Letter to You

Today — the day the universe gave you to the world
My darling,

There are not enough words in every language combined to describe what you mean to me. You are the reason mornings feel gentle, the reason stars seem closer, the reason a simple Tuesday can hold an entire lifetime of beauty.

I think about the exact moment I knew — not suspected, not hoped, but knew — that you were it for me. It wasn't dramatic. It was quiet. It was you, laughing at something small, completely unaware of what you were doing to my heart.

On this day — the day you came into this world — I am not just celebrating your birthday. I am celebrating every version of you that existed before me, every version I have had the privilege of loving, and every version yet to come that I cannot wait to know.

You carry a warmth that changes rooms. You have a laugh I would cross oceans just to hear once more. And in those quiet, ordinary moments — the ones no one else sees — that is where I fall for you, over and over, without trying.

So today belongs to you. All of it. Every candle, every wish, every heartbeat on this page — it is all yours. Because you, my love, deserve nothing less than everything.

Forever yours, in every lifetime 💕
